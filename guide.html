Room Architecture

Importance of Room Conditions

  • The Area Roof should Cover with Coconut Leaf. Because it reduces temperature, which is good for silkworms.
  • Plant many Neem trees Near the Spot. It is good for the worms.
  • Don't use cement Roof.
  • Design the Area With Many Windows.

Preparation for Equipments

Select the perfect Needs for cultivation

Type 1 - BAMBOO RACK

  • The Bamboo racks are highly resistant to heat.
  • There is no need to cover with any other materials.
  • There is no need for plastic nets.

Type 2 - Steel Rack

  • It is safer than bamboo racks, but it does not resist heat as well.
  • Need to cover the racks with papers.
  • We should use Plastic nets for Cocoon cultivation.

Silkworm Cultivation

GROWTH OF SILKWORM

Harvesting Silk

Harvesting silk from matured silkworms

  • The ripe worms usually crawl down the base of the tree during dusk.
  • A matured worm can be distinguished by a rustling sound when put near one's ear and rubbed with a finger.
  • The worms are collected in a bamboo basket.
  • 40 worms are mounted per square foot.
  • Harvest cocoons only after complete pupation.

Silk Processing

  • Sericulture. This is the term used to describe the process of gathering the silkworms and harvesting the cocoon to collect the materials.
  • Thread extraction
  • Dyeing
  • Spinning
  • Weaving
  • Finishing
